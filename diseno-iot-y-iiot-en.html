How the device communicates

We choose the wireless protocol based on range, power consumption, and the environment it will operate in.

Wi-Fi & BLE

For consumer products with access to a home network or a nearby phone.

LoRa

Long range and low power, useful when there's no Wi-Fi available on site.

Wired / RS-485

When the connection needs to be reliable in industrial environments with interference.

03 — Industrial IIoT

Consumer IoT vs. industrial IIoT

It's not the same design: a connected appliance and a plant-floor sensor have very different requirements.

IoT — Consumer product

  • Connects to home Wi-Fi or a mobile app via BLE
  • Low cost per unit, volume production
  • Battery cycle designed for daily use

IIoT — Industrial environment

  • Robust protocols like Modbus or RS-485
  • Resistance to electrical noise, dust, and vibration
  • Remote monitoring for predictive maintenance
