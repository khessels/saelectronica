01 — Platforms

Microcontrollers we work with

Each platform has its own strengths; we choose the one that best fits your project, not the one that's most convenient for us.

PlatformUsed whenPossible approaches
ESP32 The project needs Wi-Fi, BLE, or more processing headroom. Bare metal C, Arduino, MicroPython
STM32F (F4 series and similar) Precise real-time control or advanced peripherals are required. Bare metal C at register/HAL level
ATmega (classic Arduino) The project is simple, low-cost, and doesn't need connectivity. Bare metal C, Arduino

02 — Programming approaches

Bare metal, Arduino, or MicroPython

The language and framework are chosen based on how performance-critical the project is and how quickly you need a working prototype.

Bare metal C

Direct register-level programming, with no intermediate layers. Full control over the hardware and maximum performance.

Arduino

Faster development using the framework and its libraries, ideal for prototypes and medium-complexity projects.

ESP32 · ATmega

MicroPython
