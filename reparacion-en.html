01 — What we repair most

Home electronics and computers

Diagnosis and repair with original or certified equivalent parts.

Televisions

LED, LCD, and smart TVs — screen, power supply, main board, and software.

Computers

Laptops and desktops — power supplies, screens, maintenance, and power-on faults.

Radios

Analog and digital radios, band equipment, and receivers.

Washing machines

Motors, control boards, drain pumps, and spin-cycle faults.

Refrigerators

Cooling systems, thermostats, compressors, and electronic controls.

Audio equipment

Speakers, receivers, and mini-systems — no sound, distortion, or power-on faults.
